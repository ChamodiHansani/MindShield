If MindShield detects a high-risk post, it connects people to these trusted Sri Lankan support services immediately.

Emotional Support
🕊️

Sumithrayo

"We are friends in need"

A non-profit volunteer befriending service offering confidential, non-judgmental emotional support to anyone in distress, despair, or at risk of suicide - in Sinhala, Tamil, and English.

📞 011 269 6666 🌐 sumithrayo.org

Available 24 / 7 · Completely confidential

Crisis Counselling
💛

CCCline

"Courage · Compassion · Commitment"

A free, confidential telephone counselling service by the CCC Foundation, supporting anyone facing emotional difficulties - domestic issues, mental health concerns, grief, stress, and more. Trained counsellors listen and refer to specialist services when needed.

📞 1333 🌐 cccline.org

Mon – Sun, 9 AM – 5 PM · Free & confidential
